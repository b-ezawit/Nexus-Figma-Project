Your Questions Answered, Enjoy Your Seamless Experience!

Everything you need to know about our artisanal baking process, custom cake orders, and daily treats.

When is the bread pulled fresh from the oven?

Our bakers start at 3:00 AM. Sourdough and baguettes are ready by 7:00 AM, while specialty pastries arrive in the display by 8:30 AM.

Do you take custom orders for birthday cakes?

Yes! We love creating custom cakes. We require at least 72 hours' notice for simple designs and 2 weeks for tiered wedding or event cakes.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free pastries?

We bake a selection of vegan muffins and gluten-free brownies daily. Please note our kitchen handles flour, so we are not a certified celiac-safe facility.

Do you use local and organic ingredients?

We source 100% of our flour from local mills and use organic seasonal fruits from nearby farms for all our tarts and danishes.

Can I pre-order pastries for events?

Absolutely! We welcome event pre-orders. Simply contact us at least 48 hours in advance for small orders and 1 week for large catering requests.

Do you offer baking classes or workshops?

Yes! We host monthly baking workshops where you can learn to make bread, pastries, and cakes. Check our website for upcoming dates and registration details.

How do you ensure freshness for take-home items?

All baked goods are prepared daily and stored in airtight packaging. We recommend consuming within 24-48 hours for optimal flavor and texture.
